2026 Timeline

From registration to finals.

Registration opens on 29 June 2026.

  1. 12 July 2026

    Registration Opens

    Teams of three begin registering for the first GAAC season.

  2. 30 August 2026

    Registration Closes

    Final team lists are locked before the qualification stage.

  3. 2 September 2026

    Mock Test

    An optional practice round to familiarize with the exam platform and question format.

  4. 5 September 2026

    Round 1 - Individual Qualification

    40 multiple-choice questions covering all syllabus domains. 1-hour time limit. Every member takes the exam individually; scores contribute to the team total.

  5. 10 September 2026

    Round 2 - Team Challenge

    10 problem-solving questions: 3 Observational Astronomy, 4 Astrophysics, 2 Cosmology, 1 Planetology. Teams collaborate on a shared submission.

  6. 15 September 2026

    Final Results & Certificates

    Winners are announced, and all participants receive their certificates of achievement.

Global Astronomy & Astrophysics Challenge, organized by STEM Astronomy Club.
