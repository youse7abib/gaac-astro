Competition Format

Individual qualification, then collaborative knockouts.

Round 1 - 5 Sep

Individual Qualification

Each participant competes individually for 1 hour across 40 multiple-choice questions. The three scores are combined, and the top teams advance to Round 2.

Round 2 - 10 Sep

Team Challenge

Teams solve 10 problem-solving questions together: 3 Observational Astronomy, 4 Astrophysics, 2 Cosmology, 1 Planetology. Submitted as a single team response.

Results - 15 Sep

Final Results & Certificates

Winners are announced and all participants receive certificates of achievement.
